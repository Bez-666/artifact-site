About

Three Decades.
One Industry.

ARTIFACT is the creative vehicle of Bez — a UK creative director and product designer based in Bangkok, with over 30 years spanning illustration, CAD engineering, industrial design, 3D art, and brand identity.

A working tattooist for over two decades — one of the very few product designers in this space who actually comes from the industry. Every brief gets filtered through real studio experience, not guesswork.

For the last 20+ years, designing products and brands for some of the tattoo industry's most recognised companies — with a specialism helping Asian manufacturers build the credibility and visual identity that Western markets respond to.
